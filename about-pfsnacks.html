Pepperidge Farm Snacks

Who we are?

PFSNACKS is a critical link in the Pepperidge Farm supply chain. We are the Philadelphia metropolitan area's exclusive Sales Development Associates for Pepperidge Farm's biscuit division, which produces the most delicious cookies, crackers and gluten-free snacks on the planet. PFSNACKS is a division of the Waxman Family Group.

What we do?

PFsnacks provides expert Direct-Store-Delivery (DSD) services to the Philadelphia metropolitan area market for Pepperidge Farm cookies, crackers and healthy On-the-Go snacks. For more than 50 years, PFsnacks has been an engine to increase sales growth for Philadelphia retailers. Our DSD services provide a variety of value added services to improve sales and margins for the retailer:

  • Shelf Inventory Management

  • In-store Forecasting

  • Store Level Authorized Item Management

  • Price and Promotion Execution

  • Store Ordering

  • In-store Merchandising

Why Direct-Store-Delivery?

The benefits of DSD over Centralized Warehouse Distribution are well-known - our in-person sales and distribution support services provide retailers with opportunities to reduce inventory carrying costs from their supply chain and simply profit from sales of popular Pepperidge Farm cookies and crackers like Milano, Chessman and GoldFish. Our DSD services provide a variety of value added services to improve sales and margins for retailer, and industry research reveals that Direct-Store-Delivery services benefit retailers as follows:

  • Increased sales because DSD vendors merchandise their products in the stores multiple times per week

  • Out of stocks are reported as being 2 - 4% less for DSD products than distribution center products

  • Shelf-tag & scan errors are reduced because DSD item data is managed by vendors and communicated to retailers

  • Reduced labor expense for reordering and merchandising products

  • Merchandising time spent handling item data is reduced by 5 - 10%

  • Bypassing distribution centers takes out 0.5 - 1.0% inventory out of the retail distributor’s inventory assets

  • Thousands of hours of retail warehouse labor hours saved

  • Logistics costs savings to the retailer are estimated at a 1.0%+ reduction.

  • A 5 - 10% reduction in finance time and audit fees spent reconciling invoices.

  • Speed to market for new items is improved by 2 weeks because products bypass retail distribution centers in the supply chain

Source: MWPVL International Consulting
